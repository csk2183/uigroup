Exceptions Explained

Welcome to the most in depth portion of learning on this platform. Learn about the exceptions of offsides in soccer!

Offsides exceptions in action:

Not in play:

Before pass

Offside line is established before the pass is made

After pass

Offside player removed themselves from interfering in the play, so the player who was not offside can legally play the ball

Half field:

A player cannot be offside in their side of the field.

If the player is behind the ball (offside line), they cannot be offside
