Exceptions Explained

Welcome to the most in depth portion of our learning platform. Learn about the exceptions of the offside rules in soccer!

Simple offside exceptions in the half field:

A player cannot be offside while on their own side of the field.

If the player is behind the ball, they cannot be offside.

More in-depth offside exception in the full field:

Offside line is established before the pass is made, meaning the far blue player is offsides, while the middle blue player is onsides.

After the pass is made, the offside player does not interfere in the play, so the player who was not offside can legally play the ball.

Offsides exceptions in action

Key moment to look at when determining offsides: interfering with the play at the moment when the ball is passed

Remember: only the team with the ball can be offside, and offside can only happen in the attacking part of the field
