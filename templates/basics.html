11 players per side: Defense (D), Midfield (M), Forward (F), Goalie (G)

What happens: Ball crosses the red lines

Result: If Team A touches the ball last, then Team B throws the ball in

What happens: Ball crosses the yellow lines

Result: If the attackers touch the ball last, then the defenders get a goal kick. Otherwise, the attackers get a corner kick.
